Topic Proposal

Problem/Data Domain

Topic Overview

COVID-19 has become quite a serious issue in the past few months, and there is a lot of information that is necessary for people to be prepared and respond to it that is not as easily availible as it should be. Therefore, it is necessary to collect and analyze more information so people, researchrs, and government officials can respond to the crisis.

Information Technology Problem

How can we use data to demonstrate the way that COVID-19 cases have increased across the different states in the United States?

Past Solutions to this Problem

Past solutions for showcasing the distrubution of COVID-19 cases in the United States include several distrubution maps made by newspapers like the New York Times. Additionally, state governments provide regularly updated statistics on the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases and casulties.

Users

The users of this application will be anyone who is interested in learning more about the spread of COVID-19 across the United States. This well help these people get a better understanding of how COVID-19 has impacted their location over time, and understand the state- wide distribution of COVID-19 cases.

Information Sources

For this application, I will draw on data in a .CSV file from the New York Times' COVID-19 case count article. This dataset includes statistics on the number of confirmed cases, casulties, and tests excecuted in the United States. This will give me the data to generate interactive state-based graphics that the users can explore to get a better understanding of the distrubtion of COVID-19 in their state over time.

UI

The primary interactions that the users will be able to explore are:

  • View COVID-19 data according to different states
  • View COVID-19 data over a selected timeframe

Solution

This application will solve a problem by providing more accessible and interactive information about COVID-19 cases in individual states of the United States, which will help provide a better understanding of how COVID-19 has impacted states over time.
